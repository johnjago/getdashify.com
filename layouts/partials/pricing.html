FAQs

Do I need to buy additional licenses for staging, development, or local sites?

No. A single Dashify Pro license can be activated on an unlimited number of test sites, in addition to one live site. We detect known staging domains, so if it doesn’t, please get in touch with us to add a new staging site exception.

Can I use Dashify Pro on a staging site without an active license?

No. You’ll still need an active license to use Dashify Pro on a staging site.

Is there a lifetime deal?

At the moment, we only offer monthly pricing, but we’re planning to introduce a one time payment once the Dashify theme is fully complete across all of WooCommerce.

Can I change the number of licenses after purchase?

Yes. From your account dashboard, you can increase or decrease the number of licenses.
